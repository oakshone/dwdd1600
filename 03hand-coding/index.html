Karelia

Skiing dates back to 6000 BCE in a region in western Russia on the border of Finland. The sport evolved from the beginnings in Scandinavia, starting in the mid-1800's skiing became very popular in snow regions world wide at that time. The word skiing comes from Old Norse word which means "cleft wood" or "stick of wood" or as it is know now by ski.
